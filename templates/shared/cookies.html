Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us make the site work better for you by:

  • Remembering your settings and preferences
  • Understanding how you use our site
  • Improving your user experience

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Functionality Cookies

Remember choices you make to improve your experience.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Updates to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
